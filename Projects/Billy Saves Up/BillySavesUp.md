### The Situation
**Billy** decides spending all his money on **Halloween candy** isn't the smartest thing to do, so he decides to start **saving up money**. His parents, happy about Billy's newfound maturity, decide to give him **a dollar every day**. And **Casper**, being the friendly ghost he is, decides to also **double his money every day**.

**The Question:** How many days will it take for Billy to become a **billionaire**?

---

### Your Task
While it is possible to do this mathematically, we want you to use your new skills of **for loops** and **while loops** to figure out how many days it'll take!

---

**Hint:** A billionaire has **\$1,000,000,000**. You'll need to set up a loop that tracks Billy's money and the number of days until the total reaches or exceeds this amount.
